This demo shows how the Identity Provider permission screens can also be shown in "full-screen" mode if that is preferred.

To test more than once you will need to force the IDPs to "forget" your permissions for this site.
(example for Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications)
(example for Google: https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions)

In both pages, find our test application ("janrain-se-demo") and remove it.
