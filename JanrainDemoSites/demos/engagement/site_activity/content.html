Site Activity Feed

Display the activities consumers participate in while on your site, such as comments and shares, in real-time social feeds. Let consumers filter activities by the individual, friends from social networks, or all site users, so they can see which content is engaging the rest of your community.

Note that this demo is based on the Backplane 1.2 specification. For more details, see:
http://backplanex.com/specification-documents/backplane-specification-1-2/

Try viewing the source to see what's going on.
