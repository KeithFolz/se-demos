Leader Board

This is a demo for Janrain Enterprise with Leader Board. Integrate a visualization of clickable trends or celebrities, such as athletes and artists, showing the total number real-time updates of public and private social media mentions. Great for on-air display during live broadcast events or for demonstrating trending topics and celebrities in a visual way.

Note that this demo is based on the Backplane 1.2 specification. For more details, see:
http://backplanex.com/specification-documents/backplane-specification-1-2/

Try viewing the source to see what's going on.


Click on a name or an image to change what the stream below shows.
