Talent QnA

Drive customer engagement during high-traffic events by enabling real-time talent QnA. Consumers can interact with the talent and voice opinions during high-profile events, TV programs, webcasts, and more.

Try out these features on this example implementation:
  • After signing into site, you are also signed into Talent QnA
  • Like a question
  • Share your question to Facebook and Twitter (if logged in with that IDP)
  • Dirty word filter prevents offensive posts
  • Questions added on this page also show in Site Activity Feed
  • Users can delete their own questions

Note that this demo is based on the Backplane 1.2 specification. For more details, see:
http://backplanex.com/specification-documents/backplane-specification-1-2/
